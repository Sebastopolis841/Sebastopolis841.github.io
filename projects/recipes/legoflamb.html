Roast leg of lamb

Info about food

Ingredients

  • 6 lb. leg of lamb
  • At least 6 cloves of garlic, sliced
  • 1 tbsp of dried oregano
  • 4 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 lemons, cut in half

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450˚
  2. Wash and dry leg of lamb
  3. With the point of a sharp knife, make small slits in both sides of the lamb on all sides
  4. Insert garlic slices in the slits
  5. Squeeze and rub each lemon half as well as the olive oil over the leg and leave the lemons in the pan
  6. Season with salt, pepper and oregano then place fat side up in pan
  7. Roast for 1/2 an hour at 450˚, then reduce heat to 350˚ and roast for an additional 1 and 1/2 hours

Makes 6 - 8 servings
