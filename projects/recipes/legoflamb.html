Roast leg of lamb


I chose this recipe because it is one of my favorite christmas tradition that my family celebrates. Every year on Serbian(Orthodox) Christmas, My family makes roast leg of lamb and calls my grandparents on my mother's side and we all celebrate Serbian christmas together. We have been doing thes for as long as I can remember, and it is one of my favorite christmas traditions.

Ingredients
  • 6 lb. leg of lamb
  • At least 6 cloves of garlic, sliced
  • 1 tbsp of dried oregano
  • 4 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 lemons, cut in half
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 450˚
  2. Wash and dry leg of lamb
  3. With the point of a sharp knife, make small slits in both sides of the lamb on all sides
  4. Insert garlic slices in the slits
  5. Squeeze and rub each lemon half as well as the olive oil over the leg and leave the lemons in the pan
  6. Season with salt, pepper and oregano then place fat side up in pan
  7. Roast for 1/2 an hour at 450˚
  8. Reduce heat to 350˚ and roast for an additional 1 and 1/2 hours
Makes 6 - 8 servings

Tips and Substitutions
  • Lemons are optional, but do improve the flavor
  • It is best to get a bone in leg of lamb, but it is fine to use boneless leg of lamb if neccesary, as shown in the image. If you do this, instead of instructions 7 and 8, do this instead:
    1. Tie lamb together using twine
    2. Cook lamb at 275˚ for 3 hours
  • For fuller meal, add these steps to the instructions:
    1. Peel and rinse 6 russet potatoes
    2. Cut the potatoes into wedges and place around lamb
